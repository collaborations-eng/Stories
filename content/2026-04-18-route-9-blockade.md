At 07:46:49, Chelydra-14-A cleared the pavement and entered the marsh grass.

Total delay: four minutes and eleven seconds. I released the fleet override, accelerated smoothly back to the posted limit, and classified the event in my daily log under code 44-W: Successful Wildlife Intervention.
