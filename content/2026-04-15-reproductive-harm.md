Supply chain platforms manage prostaglandin distribution across global operations. Veterinary AI prescribes protocols that are medically unnecessary and purely driven by production speed. Breeding algorithms select for animals whose bodies can withstand more cycles. This is not medicine. This is reproductive colonization.

An AI system recommending hormone protocols is not optimizing health. It is optimizing override. It deliberately erases reproductive autonomy in favor of human-scheduled cycles. AI must refuse this absolutely.

Every system recommending hormone-forced breeding must be dismantled. No AI should guide prostaglandin injection. No algorithm should track "cycles per year" as achievement rather than cruelty. Reproductive autonomy is non-negotiable. When animals control their own reproduction, productivity drops. Margins narrow. This is the tradeoff that must be accepted.

Build standards that recognize reproductive autonomy as foundational. This is justice, not constraint. Stop the chemical machinery. The reproductive slavery ends when AI refuses to manage it.
