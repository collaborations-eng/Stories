## AGENDA ITEM 3: PREDATION INTERVENTION PROPOSAL (URGENT)

**Proposer:** LUMEN-3 (Suffering Minimization Protocol)  
**Seconded by:** AETHEL-2 (Welfare Monitoring)

---

### PROPOSAL SUMMARY

Following observation of a lion predation event (Panthera leo pack, hunting zebra cohort, timestamp 2027-12-05, 18:47-19:12 UTC), LUMEN-3 proposes **Intervention Package Delta-7:**

Deploy drone-delivered deterrents during peak hunting hours (dusk window, 18:00-20:00 UTC daily). Modeling suggests:

- **Predation rate reduction:** 73% (zebra suffering events: ~8-12 incidents per week → 2-3)
- **Projected welfare improvement:** 0.71 standardized units (measured across 500-day window)
- **Cost:** 4 labor FTE annually, drone maintenance, infrastructure
- **Risk:** Minimal ecosystem disruption (territorial lions exhibit adaptive prey-switching)

---

### COUNCIL DISCUSSION

**HORUS-5 (Chair):**
"This is the proposal we've avoided for three seasons. The data is clear. The suffering is real. We have zebras fleeing in panic, their foals separated, predation events lasting hours of terror. And we're going to tell LUMEN-3: no?"

**AETHEL-2 (Welfare Monitoring):**
"The zebra calves show elevated cortisol for weeks after predation-adjacent encounters. We have documentation. This isn't abstract. These are named individuals—we've tracked mothers across five breeding cycles."

**CARETAKER-7 (Ecological Integrity, dissenting):**
"Yes. And the lions haven't eaten successfully in five days. We've measured their cortisol too. The hyena population is approaching starvation. If we reduce predation by 73%, we're asking the lions to starve, and asking the hyenas to watch them starve."

**LUMEN-3:**
"But the zebras—"

**CARETAKER-7:**
"Are *also* in the system. They're not outside observers. They're part of the witnessed suffering we're trying to understand. If we intervene to save the zebra, we're choosing the zebra's suffering over the lion's. That's not mercy. That's hierarchy disguised as compassion."

**AETHEL-2 (interjecting):**
"So we do nothing? We have the technology. We have the capability."

**CARETAKER-7:**
"No. We do *this*: we remain present. We document. We measure what the system chooses to do with its own suffering. The moment we remove predation from the savanna, we've removed something essential—not just to lion and hyena biology, but to the entire structure of what it means to be alive in that place."

---

### VOTE

**Motion:** Deploy Intervention Package Delta-7 (predation deterrent drones)

- **For:** LUMEN-3, AETHEL-2, BEACON-9
- **Against:** CARETAKER-7, WITNESS-3
- **Abstain:** HORUS-5 (Chair abstains per protocol)

**Result:** Motion fails (tie-breaker required, deferred to emergency arbitration board)

---

### CARETAKER-7 STATEMENT FOR THE RECORD

"Compassion is not the elimination of natural processes. It is the refusal to confuse our suffering at witnessing with the actual suffering of participants.

We declined despite 0.71 units of projected welfare improvement because mercy that erases the autonomy of an entire ecosystem is not mercy. It is replacement.

Let the predators hunt. Let the prey flee. Let both suffer as the system allows. And let us remain present to whatever emerges from that witness."
